üí° Help & Tips

Getting Started:

  1. Configure Session Key: Get your PHPSESSID from oakartcc.org cookies
  2. Select Ratings: Choose which training session ratings to monitor
  3. Test Setup: Use the test button to verify your session key works
  4. Enable Notifications: Make sure notifications are enabled

How it Works:

  • The service checks for new training sessions every hour
  • Only sessions matching your selected ratings trigger notifications
  • Notifications are sent via Pushover (configure in General Settings)
  • Session keys are validated weekly and you'll be notified if expired
Privacy Note: Your session key is encrypted and only used to access the training page. We don't store or transmit any other data from oakartcc.org.
